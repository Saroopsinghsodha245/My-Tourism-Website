Discover Umerkot – A Land of History & Culture

Umerkot District (Amarkot) lies in southeastern Sindh, Pakistan. Known for its deep cultural heritage, it's the birthplace of Mughal Emperor Akbar and a unique mix of Hindu-Muslim heritage.

Umerkot Fort, Shri Shiv Mandir, and the folk tale of Umar Marvi make this desert town a living storybook of history and culture.
